Caffe Latte.

A latte is the ultimate comfort in a cup—a harmonious balance of bold espresso and velvety steamed milk. Known for its smooth, creamy texture and finished with a delicate layer of airy microfoam, it offers a mellow, indulgent flavor that is perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • Espresso: 1–2 shots
  • Milk: 6–10 oz of whole milk
  • Microfoam
  • A pump of caramel

Instructions

  1. Brew Espresso: Extract 1–2 shots of espresso into a large mug
  2. Steam Milk: Heat 6–10 oz of milk to approximately 65°C (150°F)
  3. Aerate: Froth the milk briefly to create a silky, thin layer of microfoam
  4. Pour: Slowly pour the steamed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam with a spoon
  5. Top: Finish by layering the remaining microfoam on top
